About Us

Michael G. D’Annunzio relocated from Philadelphia, PA to Brigantine, NJ in 1977, after having a successful building career specializing in medical buildings and residential construction. After several years of achieving a notable career in design and construction, Michael was elected by his peers to the office of President of the Atlantic Builders Association in 1984-1985. In 1985, he served as a Board of Director for the New Jersey State Builders Association.

For the past four decades he has worked with hundreds of homeowners in South Jersey where he built a very high reputation for honesty, longevity, and quality workmanship. We pride ourselves on completing our projects on or before schedule and offer an extended two-year warranty. References are always available.
